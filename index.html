Experience the World Without Leaving Your Seat

Virtual Voyages delivers immersive, therapeutic, and exciting virtual reality (VR) sessions to retirement communities. With lightweight Meta Quest headsets, residents can visit world landmarks, relax in nature, and connect with loved ones — all from their seats.

Our Services

  • Virtual Travel: Tour iconic sites like Paris, Machu Picchu, and the Grand Canyon.
  • Relaxation & Meditation: Enjoy calming beaches, gardens, and guided wellness.
  • Cognitive Wellness: Engage memory and thinking with light games and interactions.
  • Family & Social Connection: Meet virtually with family in the Alcove VR lounge.

Each session is 100% guided and tailored to your residents. We provide all equipment, setup, and assistance. Residents stay seated, and all gear is sanitized between uses. No tech knowledge needed—just wonder and curiosity.

Why Communities Choose Virtual Voyages

  • Designed specifically for seniors with guided sessions
  • Safe, seated, and fully sanitized equipment
  • Encourages joy, social bonding, and reminiscence
  • Perfect for assisted living, independent living, and memory care
